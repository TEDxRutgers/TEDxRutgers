Who We're Looking For

At TEDxRutgers, we search year-round for presenters who will inform and inspire, surprise and delight. Our presenters run admired companies and design its best-loved products; they invent world-changing devices and create ground-breaking media. They are trusted voices and convention-breaking mavericks, icons and geniuses.
